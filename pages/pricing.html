Advisory Policies

Billing & Travel Protocols

Transparent terms covering consultants' travel, credit charges, and NDA alignment.

Travel & Logistical Expenses

Domestic and international flights or lodgings for on-site workshops are billed at cost plus a 5% handling surcharge, or aligned with client corporate travel budgets.

Secure Retainer Cancellations

Retainer partnerships can be terminated with a 30-day written notice. Any remaining credit balance is refunded, minus the current month's audit hours.

Billing FAQs

Pricing Inquiries

Commonly asked financial board questions regarding pricing structures and success parameters.

For M&A deals, success fees are calculated as a small percentage of the final transaction size (typically using the Lehman scale), invoiced only upon successful deal closing.

Up to 15% of unused monthly retainer advisory hours can be rolled over to the next billing cycle, expiring after 60 days to prevent structural backlogs.
